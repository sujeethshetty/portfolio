### Step-by-Step: Craft Your AI Video Commercial

#### **Scene & Setting**

-   Choose an indoor/outdoor environment and time of day.
-   Highlight brand-relevant props or backdrops.

#### **Camera & Composition**

-   Specify movement (e.g., “slow crane-down”) and shot types.
-   Use rule-of-thirds framing for visual interest.

#### **Audio Design**

-   Outline dialogue lines and tone.
-   Add ambient noise (city hum, café chatter) and subtle SFX (notification pings).
-   Pick a music loop that matches your brand mood.

#### **Overlay & Branding**

-   Include a semi-transparent watermark (e.g., `yourapp.com`) throughout.
-   Pop up a lower-third infobox when your app is mentioned.

#### **Style & Finish**

-   Call out lighting (golden hour, overcast) and depth of field.
-   End on a wide shot with your logo fade-in.

### Your Fill-in-the-Blanks Veo 3 Prompt Template

```yaml
Scene: A {indoor/outdoor} setting at {time of day}, e.g., a {modern office/rooftop terrace} with {city/landscape} view.  
Two characters—{CHAR1} and {CHAR2}—interact naturally, holding {prop}.  

CAMERA  
• Start with {camera movement} to a {shot type}.  
• Cut to a {close-up} on key dialogue.  

AUDIO  
• Ambient: {background sounds}.  
• Music: {mood, e.g., warm piano loop}.  
• SFX: subtle “{notification sound}” when {AppName} is mentioned.  

**{CHAR1}** ({tone})  
“{Question about the app’s insight or alert}?”

**{CHAR2}** ({tone})  
“{AppName} did—it {real-time alert feature} so I {benefit}.”  
*(Soft notification SFX)*  

---

**ON-SCREEN TEXT & OVERLAY**  
• Watermark: semi-transparent “{app_url}” at bottom-center.  
• Infobox on “{AppName}” mention:  
  “Real-time AI-powered Financial News alerts”  

---

STYLE & POST  
• {Lighting style, e.g., natural golden hour}.  
• Depth of field: {shallow focus on characters}.  
• End with {wide shot} and {AppName} logo fade-in.
```
