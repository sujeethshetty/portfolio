### **The Engineering Challenge**

On May 21st, NVIDIA announced a partnership with Navitas Semiconductor to develop 800V HVDC architecture for next-generation data centers. Within 72 hours, NVTS rallied 300%. The interesting part? Most traders found out after the fact.

This got me thinking about the fundamental problem in financial news delivery: it’s not about having more information — it’s about having the right information at the right time, with proper context.

### **Introducing the MCP Server: AI-Powered Financial News Assistant**

I’m excited to share a major milestone in TezNewz’s evolution — the **introduction of the MCP Server**, an innovative Model Context Protocol implementation that’s changing how the financial news is delivered. While still in the early stages of development, this intelligent system represents my vision for the future of financial information access.

The MCP Server is my first step toward creating truly intelligent financial news delivery. By combining artificial intelligence with real-time data processing, I’m building a system that goes beyond traditional news feeds, one that understands your intent and provides contextual, actionable insights.

### **What Makes this MCP Server Different?**

#### **Intelligent Query Understanding**

Gone are the days of rigid keyword searches. The MCP Server leverages **OpenAI’s GPT-4o** to understand natural language queries with remarkable precision. Whether you ask for **_Tesla earnings news_**, **_TSLA updates_**, or **_What’s happening with electric vehicle stocks_**, the system intelligently extracts your intent and delivers relevant results.

The magic happens through advanced ticker extraction algorithms that I developed to handle various formats seamlessly. From **_Apple (AAPL)_** to **_NASDAQ: TSLA_**, the system recognizes and processes different ticker representations with confidence scoring to ensure accuracy.

### **Architecture Overview: How MCP Made TezNewz Agentic**

Traditional news platforms are reactive ; they wait for you to search. We wanted TezNewz to be proactive, understanding intent before you even finish typing. MCP enabled this transformation by providing:

1\. **Context Awareness**: The agent understands conversation history and market conditions based on the financial news it has

2\. **Tool Integration**: Seamless connection between AI decision-making and data operations

3\. **Autonomous Actions**: The ability to chain multiple operations without human intervention

### **How It All Works Together**

Our MCP-powered agent transforms how you consume financial news. Instead of scrolling through dozens of headlines, you get concise, actionable intelligence.

**Simple Command, Powerful Results:**

Type **_/news NVIDIA_** in Discord, and within seconds you receive:

-   **Latest Headlines**: All recent NVIDIA news from the past 24 hours
-   **AI Summary**: A concise paragraph explaining what’s happening and why it matters
-   **Impact Analysis**: How significant each piece of news is for traders and investors
-   **Market Context**: How this fits into broader market trends

The agent doesn’t just dump raw headlines — it understands which news actually moves markets and synthesizes everything into a clear, decision-ready report.
